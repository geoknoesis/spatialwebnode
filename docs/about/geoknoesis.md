# About Geoknoesis LLC

## Company Overview

**Geoknoesis LLC** is a forward-thinking technology company specializing in spatial computing, distributed systems, and next-generation web technologies. Founded with a vision to advance the spatial web ecosystem, we develop innovative solutions that bridge the gap between traditional web technologies and spatial computing paradigms.

Our name "Geoknoesis" combines "Geo" (spatial/geographic) with "Knoesis" (knowledge), reflecting our expertise in spatial knowledge systems and intelligent spatial computing solutions.

## Our Mission & Vision

**Mission**: To advance spatial computing technologies and build robust, scalable solutions that enable the next generation of distributed spatial applications, making spatial data and location-aware computing accessible to developers and organizations worldwide.

**Vision**: To be a leading force in the spatial web revolution, creating the foundational technologies that will power the future of location-aware, distributed computing systems.

## hstpd - Our Flagship Project

**hstpd** (HSTP daemon) is our flagship implementation of the Hypermedia Spatial Transport Protocol (HSTP). This project represents our commitment to:

- **Open Standards**: Implementing and contributing to open protocols and specifications
- **Modular Architecture**: Building extensible and maintainable systems
- **Performance**: Optimizing for speed, efficiency, and scalability
- **Security**: Ensuring robust and secure implementations
- **Interoperability**: Supporting multiple transport protocols and standards
- **Developer Experience**: Providing excellent documentation and tooling

### Key Features of hstpd
- **Multi-Transport Support**: HTTP, MQTT, P2P, and extensible transport layers
- **Decentralized Identity**: Built-in DID support and management
- **Spatial Operations**: Native support for spatial computing operations
- **Container Ready**: Docker and Kubernetes deployment support
- **Native Performance**: GraalVM native image compilation
- **Production Ready**: Enterprise-grade reliability and monitoring
